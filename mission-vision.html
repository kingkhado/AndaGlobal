    Driving Excellence and Innovation in Construction

    Our Mission

    With every construction project we make it our mission to build, create & innovate as if it is our very own. With every day of operations, Anda Global lays the stones to introduce an ultra modern definition of the civil construction industry; one that's entirely designed for clients, result-oriented, and provides personalised solutions for a lap of luxury for every client, regardless of the size of the project at hand. With Anda Global, no project is too substantial or too small scale. Our mission is to provide customers with a hassle-free, one-stop construction solution at affordable rates. Experience convenience and quality like never before.

    Foresight Vision

    With the aim to drive future economic empowerment & as a catalyst for change, we hope to create opportunities, promote inclusivity, and uplift marginalised communities. Through partnerships, support for local businesses, and training programs, we empower individuals and drive sustainable economic growth.

    We at Anda Global aspire to become lifelong construction partners for every client we do business with, including the customer, design professionals, sub-contractors, and tradesmen, through our sustained provision of trailblazing construction & engineering services. By heightening our capacities and capabilities, we aim to showcase the power of contracting engineers when it comes to applying technical expertise to practical problems, not only in South Africa but Africa at large. Introducing African Engineering Skills to the world at large, We know the immense potential of African engineering skills. This will be achieved by empowering our team members and fostering continuous learning, we aim to be at the forefront of engineering innovation in the southern hemisphere.

    Our Objectives

    Creating Meaningful Employment

    Our goal is to provide full-time employment to at least 500 individuals by the year 2030. By offering stable and rewarding career opportunities, we contribute to economic growth and upliftment within our communities.

    Exceeding Project Supervision Expectations

    With a focus on meeting the project supervision needs of our customers, we ensure meticulous attention to detail, efficient coordination, and superior quality control. Our commitment is to deliver exceptional results that surpass expectations.

    Partnering for Success

    We believe in the power of collaboration. By forming partnerships and joint ventures with experienced companies, we can undertake large scale projects with confidence, leveraging our collective expertise for optimal results.

    Empowering Small Businesses

    We actively engage in subcontracting with small companies, aiming to empower them and foster their growth. By providing opportunities, support, and resources, we contribute to the development and sustainability of these enterprises.

    Empowering Previously Disadvantaged Groups

    We are dedicated to the growth and development of previously disadvantaged groups by providing skills training and opportunities. Through empowerment initiatives, we strive to create a more inclusive and equitable society.

    Leading in Civil Construction and EPWP Programs

    Anda Global Management aims to be the premier partner in civil construction and labor intensive programs under the National Department of Public Works (EPWP). With our expertise, innovation, and commitment to excellence, we drive positive change, uplift communities, and deliver exceptional results.
